This post demonstrates two approaches to embedding Claude artifacts.

---

## Option A: Direct iframe embed (full-width)

Just paste the Claude embed code directly in your MDX. This one breaks out of the content width for a full desktop experience:

<div style={{marginLeft: 'calc(-50vw + 50%)', marginRight: 'calc(-50vw + 50%)', width: '100vw', padding: '0 2rem'}}>
  <iframe
    src="https://claude.site/public/artifacts/2a378267-bc36-4858-942d-bf0815fdff85/embed"
    width="100%"
    height="700"
    style={{border: '1px solid #e5e7eb', borderRadius: '8px', maxWidth: '1400px', margin: '0 auto', display: 'block'}}
    title="Interactive Artifact"
    frameBorder="0"
    allow="clipboard-write"
    sandbox="allow-scripts allow-same-origin allow-forms allow-popups allow-modals"
  />
</div>

**Pros:** Fully visible, interactive right in the page, full-width on desktop
**Cons:** Takes up space, can't read around it

---

## Option B: Bracket syntax with registry

Using the bracket system: click [[Interactive Tool:demo-artifact]] to open in a fullscreen panel.

The bracket syntax keeps your content clean and readable. Click to expand.

**Pros:** Clean content, fullscreen panel experience
**Cons:** Requires a click to see

---

## Which to use?

- **Option A** for hero artifacts you want front-and-center
- **Option B** for supplementary tools readers can explore optionally
